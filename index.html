Mehmet Aydın Baytaş

Mehmet Aydın Baytaş is a graduate fellow at Koç University's Graduate School of Social Sciences and Humanities and a founding member of the Koç University Design Lab.

His current research involves experimenting with novel media and human-computer interaction styles involving computer vision, machine learning, gestural interaction, and sound.
