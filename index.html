Mehmet Aydın Baytaş

Mehmet Aydın Baytaş is a researcher at Chalmers University's t2i Interaction Laboratory and a graduate fellow at Koç University's Graduate School of Social Sciences and Humanities, where he took part in founding the Koç University Design Lab and the Koç University - Arçelik Research Center for Creative Industries.

His current research research involves experimenting with novel media and human-computer interaction styles to improve people's lives, drawing on computer vision, motion capture, machine learning, gestural interaction, and sound.

At night, he turns into a musician and DJ.
