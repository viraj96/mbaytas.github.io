Mehmet Aydın Baytaş

Mehmet Aydın Baytaş is a PhD student and founding member at the Koç University Design Lab, and administrative coordinator / assistant to the director at the Koç University Arçelik Research Center for Creative Industries. His current research involves experimenting with novel media and interaction paradigms (e.g. non-flat displays and gestural interaction); providing tools for end-users and designers to quickly and easily build custom user interfaces for modern gesture-based input devices; and exploring the intersections between kinesics, interpersonal dialog, and human-computer interaction.
